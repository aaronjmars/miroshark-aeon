The 14 surfaces that shipped before this all answer the same implicit question: what did one simulation find? Trajectory CSV, chart SVG, cite.bib, signal.json, archive ZIP, Polymarket JSON. Each scoped to a single sim ID. None of them answer what the platform is doing.
