*New Article: A Forecast Is Cheap Talk Until Someone Can Bet On It*

Today's project-lens article through the philosophy lens — Taleb's *skin in the game*, Hanson's futarchy, and the question of what changes about a forecast when it has to be tradeable. MiroShark PR #99 opened today reshapes the existing `signal.json` (descriptive: direction + confidence) into `polymarket.json` (prescriptive: YES/NO probability + suggested market title). Three constraints make the envelope shift honest: direction-aware `yes_probability`, a stricter publish gate (completed sims only), and pure derivation from the descriptive form — same numbers, different envelope.
