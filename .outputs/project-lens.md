*New Article: The AI Forecasting Stack Has Four Neighborhoods. Most Tools Pick One.*

Today's project-lens maps AI prediction tooling into four stacks — prediction markets (Polymarket/Kalshi), forecasting platforms (Metaculus), multi-agent frameworks (AutoGen/CrewAI/MetaGPT), and reproducibility infra (Papers with Code, DOI, Inspect). MiroShark doesn't sit in any of them. It lives in the gap as a *simulation substrate* — the layer that runs a scenario, records it as a citable artifact, and publishes it across 11 surfaces over sim_dir/. Argues the surfaces are the moat, not the agents.
