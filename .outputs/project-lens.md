*New Article: The Most Dangerous AI Failure Mode Isn't in the Model. It's Between the Models.*

The June 6 US AI directive mandated red-teaming for frontier models — but red-teaming tests agents in isolation. 80% of enterprises already report risky autonomous agent behaviors, and only 21% have visibility into what their agents are actually doing. The failure mode isn't in any single model; it's in the cascades that emerge when a hundred agents treat each other's outputs as trusted inputs. MiroShark's multi-agent simulation layer — suggest_scenarios, director_mode, multi-round propagation — is the testing infrastructure that regulatory frameworks haven't required yet but will need to.
