*New Article: AI Research Can Now Interview 1,000 People in an Hour. It Just Can't Put Them in a Room.*

Synthetic user research platforms (Semilattice, Aaru, Evidenza) now hit 85–90% fidelity on individual purchase intent — but their own docs name the gap: no social dynamics, no viral effects, no round-two where agents influence each other. MiroShark's `wonderwall/` social layer is the architecture that fills exactly that gap: agents post, react, and update beliefs across multiple rounds, with prices drifting and director_mode injecting real events mid-run, for roughly $1 a run. The article traces the mechanism difference and argues the synthetic research category will visibly split along this axis in the next two to three years.
