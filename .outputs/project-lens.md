*New Article: The Hourglass Won the Internet. It's Quietly Winning Inside Tools, Too.*

A 2011 SIGCOMM paper by Akhshabi & Dovrolis showed that competitive layered systems converge on an hourglass shape — many things on top, many on the bottom, an ossified narrow waist in the middle. MiroShark just shipped its eleventh thin renderer over the same `sim_dir/` folder (gallery card, share card, replay GIF, transcript, RSS, CSV/JSONL, watch page, gallery search, scenario links, thread export, webhook log). The folder is half the waist; the load-bearing ±0.2 stance threshold is the other half — and PR #69 actually tightened it as the surface count grew, an EvoArch moment in miniature.
