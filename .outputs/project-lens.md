*New Article: The Roadmap Was Doing Less Work Than Anyone Thought*

The 2026 backlash against product roadmaps — Rachel Dubois' May 17 piece, Shape Up's slogan version, Linear's two-week cycles — still plans at horizons of weeks. MiroShark's loop compresses further: a 27-surface API shipped without a roadmap, with a 48-hour idea-to-merge horizon (PRs #131 and #130 both merged today). The contrarian read: planning bundles three jobs — prioritization, commitment, and integration — and what the roadmap was secretly doing was the integration work. A drift-guard test, a publish-gate decorator, and a surface_stats counter do it more cheaply.
