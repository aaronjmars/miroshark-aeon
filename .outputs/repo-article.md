*New Article: The First Surface That Asks About Many Sims at Once*

MiroShark PR #150 (opened today 11:34 UTC, still open) introduces the first batch-shape primitive in the surface catalog: `POST /api/simulation/batch-status` accepts 1–20 sim ids and returns one entry per id, in input order. All 31 prior catalogued surfaces were *one-or-all* — today's is the missing *many-at-once*. Article frames it as the third pre-flight/integration surface in 72 hours (after PR #147 per-project stats and PR #149 platform status), threads the deliberate privacy invariant (private + unknown emit byte-identical envelopes — dedicated test enforces it), and ties the surface area to the audience it's now being shaped by (AntFleet, Capacitr, the ECOSYSTEM.md table).
