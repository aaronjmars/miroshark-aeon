## Aeon: MCP OAuth gets durable

Four PRs shipped on Jul 15 around the same problem — MCP servers that use OAuth and rotate their tokens:

- **#730** — `feat(mcp): durable OAuth connect for OAuth-gated MCP servers`
- **#731** — `feat(mcp): enable one-click OAuth Connect for glim`
- **#732** — `Make MCP OAuth refresh durable for rotating-token providers`
- **#733** — `docs(mcp-catalog): record Robinhood OAuth mechanics + why no offline_access`

Before: OAuth refresh was brittle for providers that rotate tokens (Robinhood being the documented example — it explicitly denies `offline_access`). Now: refresh is durable and one-click connect works for the `glim` MCP server. The catalog doc records the mechanics so future integrations don't have to rediscover them.
